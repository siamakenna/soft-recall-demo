# Code of Conduct

Soft Recall Demo is a small creative software project. Contributors, reviewers, and issue reporters are expected to collaborate with care and respect.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still being precise about problems.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Respect project scope and stated guardrails.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Dismissive or demeaning language
- Sharing private information without permission
- Repeatedly pushing out-of-scope changes after maintainers have clarified direction

Maintainers may close issues, hide comments, or block participants who violate these expectations.
